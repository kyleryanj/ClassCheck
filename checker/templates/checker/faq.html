FAQ Page


What exactly does Eagle ClassCheck do?

Eagle ClassCheck polls your tracked classes (up to 3 per phone/email) every 5 minutes and sends you a notification once it sees one of them is open.

What happens when a class opens up?

Everyone who is tracking that class will be notified and that class will be removed from their tracked list.

Important: This means that you will have to re-add the class if you were unable to get in after being notified.

Why do I need to choose between an email or phone number?

That was just a design decision on my part. If you need to switch notification methods, just fill out the Untrack Classes form and then re-do the Track Classes form with your different contact info.

Is Eagle ClassCheck an official Boston College website?

No. This website was made by me - a CS Senior at BC. It is not endorsed by BC at all.

Why make Eagle ClassCheck?

Over the past few years I've witnessed many fellow students repeatedly refreshing UIS trying to see if a class is open. Well, I've automated the process now to hopefully save people some time and frustration.

What are you doing with my contact info?

Nothing. Again, I'm just a fellow student who decided to make this tool and phone/email are the fastest methods to let you know when a class opens. I will not be spamming your email or anything like that.
